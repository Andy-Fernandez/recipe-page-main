Simple Omellete Recipe

An esay and...

Preparion time

  • Total: Aprx 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • pepper
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green pepper
  • 1/4 cup of chopped tomato

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl until blended.
  2. Stir in cheese, onion, green pepper, and tomato.
  3. Pour mixture into a greased 10-inch skillet.
  4. Cook over medium heat. As eggs set, lift edges, letting uncooked portion flow underneath.
  5. When eggs are set, fold in half.
  6. Slide omelet onto plate.
